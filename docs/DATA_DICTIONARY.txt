CityMetric — Data Dictionary
cities_features.csv / cities_clustered.csv
============================================================

IDENTIFIERS
-----------
city                        City name (string)
country                     Country name (string)
region                      Region: Asia-Pacific / Europe / Americas /
                            Middle East / Africa & Middle East
latitude                    Geographic latitude (float, degrees)
longitude                   Geographic longitude (float, degrees)
population                  City population (integer)

DIMENSION SCORES (0–10 scale, higher = better)
----------------------------------------------
affordability_score         Weighted score: salary/rent ratio, food, utilities
digital_score               Internet penetration, broadband, mobile, ISP price
startup_score               Placeholder 5.0 (no data available)
urban_score                 Urban pop %, climate comfort, city size
innovation_score            R&D spending, research output, university quality
talent_score                Education, developer density, seniority
growth_score                GDP, pop growth, high-tech exports, climate trend

COMPOSITE
---------
opportunity_index           Mean of 6 active dimension scores (0–10)

CLUSTERING
----------
cluster                     Cluster ID (0–4, integer)
cluster_name                Descriptive label:
                              0 = Established Hubs
                              1 = Balanced Cities
                              2 = Rising Stars
                              3 = Digital Leaders
                              4 = Emerging Markets
